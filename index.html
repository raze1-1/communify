At Communify, we actively work to unify communities in mobilizing a collective voice. We do this through utilizing technology to support anyone and everyone in need. As long as there is someone to help, Communify has dedicated this website to supporting displaced people across the world.

Our website has a translator that can translate 133 languages, courtesy of Google Translate.

We also have a shelter locator that finds the closest shelters and provides contact information such as emails, phone numbers, and other relevant details. It also provides the fastest route from your current location.

Here at Communify, we believe community is key. We have a forum for all users, registered or not, to allow people to access information such as potential housing opportunities, newly opened shelters, work opportunities, and more. It is required that both parties verify their identities and schedule a meeting beforehand to ensure security.

The Communify team does not tolerate any misuse of the forums, and individuals found to be in violation of forum rules will have their accounts immediately terminated.

Lastly, we have a blog where people who have successfully utilized Communify can share their experiences and tips for others who may find themselves in similar situations.
